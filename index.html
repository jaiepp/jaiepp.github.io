THE MAN BEHIND THE CAMERA


JP BARNARD
Born and raised in Greenville, South Carolina, JP experienced memory problems at a young age which led him to the miracle of photography. Taking pictures of everything helped to JP savor moments that otherwise would've been forgotten.

My Experience

JP has worked as a photographer and videographer for the past five years filming everything from classic cars, youth at summer camp, and most importantly, families and couples.

My Education

JP is on track to graduate from Bob Jones University with a degree in cinematic art, which has pushed him to excellence and attention to detail. JP knows the exact shots that will carry you throughout the years, reminding you of the memories that could easily be forgotten. Let's make some magic!
